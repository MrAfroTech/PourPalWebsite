SEPARATING FACT FROM FICTION

We've heard it all before. From venue owners concerned about disrupting their carefully crafted operations to bartenders worried about changes to their workflow. Let's clear up the misconceptions about what ezDrink actually does for your business.

MYTH: "These ordering apps are just going to replace my staff."

History has shown that new technology doesn't eliminate jobs—it creates them and empowers people to do their jobs better. Just like when personal computers entered the workplace and transformed productivity rather than replacing workers, ezDrink enhances the bar experience for everyone involved.

REALITY: ezDrink enhances your team's capabilities without replacing anyone.
➜
Staff Empowerment Tool
ezDrink removes the tedious parts of order-taking so your bartenders can focus on what they do best: creating excellent drinks and building relationships with customers. The result? Less burnout and higher job satisfaction.
➜
Potential for Increased Tips
With smoother service and preset tip options in the app, bartenders may see their average tips increase. This means your staff can potentially earn more while providing better service.
➜
Your Bartender's Favorite Bartender
ezDrink helps bartenders remember customer preferences and order history, giving guests the feeling of being seen, heard, and remembered even in busy venues. This personalized attention creates lasting connections without requiring bartenders to memorize hundreds of faces and orders.

MYTH: "We'll need to redesign our entire layout with pickup lanes."

Many venue owners worry that digital ordering systems require major renovations or dedicated pickup areas that disrupt their carefully designed spaces.

REALITY: ezDrink works with your existing layout and flow.
➜
Zero Physical Changes Required
We designed ezDrink to work with your venue exactly as it is right now. No need for construction, renovation, or rearranging your space.
➜
Simple Tablet Integration
All you need is a tablet behind the bar. Orders come in digitally where your bartenders already work, exactly like regular orders but without the shouting and chaos.
➜
Preserves Your Venue's Character
Your venue's atmosphere and aesthetic remain completely intact. ezDrink works invisibly in the background without changing what makes your place special.
  • No special hardware — works with your existing equipment
  • No dedicated pickup lanes — maintains your current service flow
  • No disruption to your vibe — preserves the atmosphere you've created

Enhanced, Not Replaced Experience

Improved Customer Experience
