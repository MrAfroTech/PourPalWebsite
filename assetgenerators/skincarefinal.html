Skincare Routine

AM Routine Section

AM

1

Wash Face

Wake up your skin by washing your face with water - use a gentle cleanser if required

2

Toner

Toner is used so your face has a balanced PH, close visible pores and makes the skin ready to use the product

3

Moisturizer w/Sunscreen

Moisturizer is a lotion or a cream to prevent the skin from getting dry and make it more smooth. Sunscreen is used to protect your skin from the sun (Ultra Violet) and from pollution

PM Routine Section

PM

1

Remove Makeup

Remove stubborn eye makeup with micellar water or oil and reusable cotton pads

2

Cleanse

Apply a gentle cleanser & wipe away with a warm, damp reusable face cloth

3

Moisturise

Slather on your moisturiser to help your skin rest & restore overnight
