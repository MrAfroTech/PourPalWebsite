Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about revolutionizing your bar service

Service & Operations

How are tips managed?
Tips can be added directly through the app with preset percentages (15%, 18%, 20%) or custom amounts. Owners maintain full control over tip distribution policies. Tips are processed separately from drink payments and can be tracked in real-time through your dashboard.
How are ages confirmed for responsible service?
Age verification remains completely in your control. Your staff checks IDs when customers pick up their orders at the bar, exactly as they do now. EzDrink doesn't change your responsible service protocols - it simply streamlines the ordering and payment process.
What happens if a drink sits too long?
Since customers order while they're already in your establishment, food/drinks are typically picked up within minutes. Orders include time stamps and real-time status updates for both customers and bartenders. If needed, you can set alerts for orders that haven't been collected within your preferred timeframe.
How does EzDrink work in high-traffic environments?
EzDrink is specifically designed for busy venues. Customers order from their phones, reducing physical crowding at the bar by up to 40%. Bartenders work more efficiently with organized digital tickets, resulting in 30% faster service and 25% increased revenue during peak hours.

Getting Started & Integration

How long does implementation take?
We tailor our offering to fit your specific bar needs, with most venues up and running within 3-5 days. Our team handles the setup, staff training, and provides on-site support during your first busy shift. All you need is a tablet for the bartender interface.
What equipment do I need?
Just a simple tablet for the bartender interface - that's it! We can provide this or you can use your existing tablet. Your customers use their own smartphones. No expensive hardware investments or complex POS integrations required!
Is staff training difficult?
Not at all! The bartender interface is intuitive and designed for speed. Most staff master it within 15 minutes. We provide comprehensive training and 24/7 support to ensure smooth operations.

Features & Benefits

What makes EzDrink different from other ordering systems?
Our AI-powered "MyTab" technology remembers customer preferences across all venues, creating a personalized VIP experience. Combined with our focus on high-volume bar environments and seamless integration, EzDrink delivers results other systems can't match.
How does the ordering process work for customers?
Customers download the EzDrink app and select your venue from the available locations. They'll see your custom menu, can customize their orders, and pay directly through the app. They receive a notification when their drink is ready. The entire process takes less than 60 seconds.
Can I customize the menu and pricing?
Absolutely! You have full control over your menu, pricing, specials, and availability. Changes update instantly across all customer devices. You can even set happy hour pricing that automatically activates at scheduled times.

Business Impact

What kind of results can I expect?
Based on our system design and market research, venues should see: 30% reduction in wait times, 25% increase in revenue, 40% improvement in staff efficiency, and significantly higher customer satisfaction scores. These projections are based on optimized order flow and reduced bar congestion.
Does this replace my bartenders?
No! EzDrink enhances your staff's abilities by eliminating time-consuming order-taking. They can focus on what they do best - making great food/drinks and creating memorable experiences. We expect this to reduce staff burnout and improve job satisfaction.

Security & Payments

How secure are customer payments?
EzDrink uses bank-level encryption and PCI-compliant payment processing. All transactions are secured with the same technology used by major financial institutions. Customer payment information is never stored on your local systems.
Can customers split tabs or pay together?
Yes! Our system supports flexible payment options including split tabs, group payments, and individual orders on shared tables. Customers can easily divide bills by items, percentages, or custom amounts directly in the app.

Customer Experience

What if customers don't have smartphones or prefer traditional ordering?
No problem! EzDrink is an additional service option, not a replacement. Customers can always order directly from your bartenders as usual. Many venues find that offering both options actually improves overall service for all customer preferences.
How do customers know when their drink is ready?
Customers receive instant notifications on their phone when their order is ready. The app also shows real-time status updates (received, in preparation, ready for pickup). For busy environments, you can also use our optional pickup display screen.
